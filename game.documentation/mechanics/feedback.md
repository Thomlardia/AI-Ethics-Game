# Player Feedback
This document shows the different ways the user could receive feedback on the decisions they have made from the stakeholders. Here, the player does not need to make a choice, just acknowledge the message. These reactions go into the stakeholders.js file.

## News Article
A public article written by the journalists, expressing the public interest

## CEO email
An email written by the CEO, expressing the trust of the board, investors, or advertisers

## Government Letter
A letter written by the government executive expressing legal concerns.
