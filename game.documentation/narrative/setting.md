# Setting

The player plays as the leader of an internal ethics task force at a large social media corporation. The company hired this task force because of much public backlash over user addiction and mental health harms of their product. The goal of the task force is to implement changes to the AI-driven recommendation system that would help reduce the addictiveness of it and improve the mental health of the user. The game takes place over several simulated “product cycles,” where player choices reshape how the platform functions and how users behave. As the leader of the internal ethical task force, you are facing the investors of the corporation, who only care about profit. It is then your task to improve the algorithm (making it less addictive), whilst dealing with the investors.

## Ethical Conflict

Reducing addictive design patterns lowers engagement and revenue, creating conflict between the people in the corporation who want better well-being for their users and the people who only seek growth and profit regardless of their users’ well-being.
